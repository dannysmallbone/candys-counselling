Supervision
I managed two projects whilst working at The Metro Centre under 25 Counselling Service and LGBTQ Hate Crime and Domestic Abuse/Violence Support Service, so I understand the importance of Clinical Supervision and I continue to provide Clinical Supervision for trainees, therapists and professionals.

Professionals who work with clients who face emotional crisis need regular support without that support their professionalism could be impacted. Professionals can receive support from their colleagues and managers but some prefer to explore their issues with an independent, external professional; a professional who can be objective who is trained to listen and to offer support.

Who can I offer support to?

  • • Qualified therapists and trainees
  • • Professionals in the helping profession

I provide a safe, confidential space where you can explore the impact of working with people who are experiencing distress and develop more effective ways of working; also a space to explore personal and professional boundaries.
